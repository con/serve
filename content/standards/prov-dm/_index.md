---
title: "PROV-DM"
description: "W3C Provenance Data Model -- standard for representing provenance information"
---

[PROV-DM](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-dm/) is the W3C standard
for representing provenance (who did what, when, using what).
Used by datalad-metalad's `metalad_runprov` extractor
to capture `datalad run` execution provenance,
and the foundation of NIDM's provenance layer.
