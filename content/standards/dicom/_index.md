---
title: "DICOM"
description: "Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine -- the standard format for medical imaging data"
---

[DICOM](https://www.dicomstandard.org/) (Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine)
is the universal format for medical imaging data from MRI, CT, PET, and other scanners.
Typically the raw input that gets converted to BIDS-organized NIfTI files
via tools like HeuDiConv/ReproIn.
